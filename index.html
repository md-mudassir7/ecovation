Acoustic CNC Panels

Acoustic CNC cut wall panels are precision-cut panels designed to improve sound absorption while adding aesthetic appeal to interior spaces. Crafted from high-density materials like MDF, plywood, or fiber cement, these panels use CNC cutting technology to create intricate patterns, perforations, or grooves that enhance acoustic performance.

Acoustic Screens

Acoustic Screens are soundproof barriers or panels designed to absorb, block, or redirect sound to reduce noise and enhance the acoustics of a space. They are ideal for offices, studios, and public areas.

Printed Acoustic Panels

Printed Acoustic Panels are a visually appealing solution that combine sound absorption with custom graphics, making them perfect for spaces where both acoustics and aesthetics matter.

Hanging Clouds

Hanging clouds, or acoustic cloud panels, provide an innovative solution for controlling sound in large spaces with high ceilings. They reduce noise, control reverberation, and enhance acoustics—ideal for concert halls, offices, classrooms, and theaters.

Acoustic Cell Ceilings

Acoustic cell ceilings consist of individual panels arranged in a grid system engineered to absorb sound and reduce reverberation. They are ideal for spaces where sound control is critical.

Acoustic Baffles

Acoustic baffles are sound-absorbing or diffusing structures used to control sound in spaces like concert halls, studios, and industrial areas. They reduce noise levels and control reverberation, creating a more controlled sound environment.
