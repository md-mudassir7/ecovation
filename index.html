Revolutionary Acoustic Panels

Advanced sound solutions made from recycled materials for superior acoustic performance

Sustainable Acoustic Innovation

By turning plastic waste, such as recycled bottles, into useful materials, Ecovation acoustic panels provide an environmentally responsible option that drastically lowers pollution and dependency on virgin plastics. They aid in the fight against climate change because their production uses less energy and produces fewer carbon emissions.

PET panels, which are well-known for their resilience and recyclable nature, prolong the life cycle of plastics and promote a circular economy. By providing efficient thermal and acoustic insulation, they also improve a building's energy efficiency.

Superior Sound Absorption

NRC ratings from 0.30 to 0.95 depending on thickness and mounting

Versatile Applications

Suitable for walls, ceilings, partition screens, furniture, and lighting

Customizable Surface

Smooth, pin-receptive, printable surface for unlimited design possibilities
