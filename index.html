About Ecovation

Inspired by nature, driven by innovation

Ecovation is a boutique interior design firm specializing in creating harmonious spaces that blend aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. Founded on the belief that good design should enhance both the environment and the human experience, we approach each project with a commitment to excellence and attention to detail.

Our Mission

To create innovative, sustainable interior spaces that enhance the quality of life for our clients while minimizing environmental impact. We strive to deliver exceptional design solutions that are both beautiful and functional.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading interior design firm that sets new standards for eco-conscious, innovative design. We aim to inspire a shift towards more sustainable practices in the industry while never compromising on style or quality.

Our Services

Tailored solutions for every space

Workplaces

Creating productive and inspiring commercial spaces that reflect your brand identity and enhance employee wellbeing. Our workplace designs optimize functionality while creating environments that inspire creativity and collaboration.

Residential

Transforming houses into personalized homes that reflect your lifestyle and aesthetic preferences. We design comfortable, functional living spaces that harmonize with your daily routines and enhance your quality of life.

Acoustic Panels

Enhancing sound quality and reducing noise with custom acoustic solutions. Our acoustic panels combine functionality with aesthetic appeal, improving the auditory experience in any space while complementing your interior design.

Space Planning

Optimizing your space for maximum efficiency and flow. Our strategic space planning ensures every square foot serves a purpose, creating intuitive layouts that enhance functionality without sacrificing style.

Sustainable Design

Integrating eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient solutions. Our sustainable design approach reduces environmental impact while creating healthy, beautiful spaces that stand the test of time.

Project Management

Overseeing your project from concept to completion. Our comprehensive project management services ensure timely execution, quality control, and seamless coordination between all parties involved.
